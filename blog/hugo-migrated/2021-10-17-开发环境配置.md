为了防止每次配置环境都要去搜索，每次配置完成之后都要重新做一些个性化设置，记录一下环境配置，一次搞定。后续会将这些配置写成一个macOS/Linux的脚本，一键配置，Windows下还是得自己手动配置。

#### 配置Java

1. 下载[JAVA_8](https://www.azul.com/downloads/?version=java-8-lts&package=jdk)、[JAVA_11](https://www.azul.com/downloads/?version=java-11-lts&package=jdk)

2. 配置环境

   JAVA_HOME: `C:\Java\jdk11`

   Path: `%JAVA_HOME%\bin;%JAVA_HOME%\jre\bin;`

   CLASSPATH: `.;%JAVA_HOME%\lib\dt.jar;%JAVA_HOME%\lib\tools.jar;`

#### 配置Android

1. [下载Toolbox](https://www.jetbrains.com/toolbox-app/)
2. 配置环境
	ANDROID_HOME: `C:\AndroidSDK`
	Path: `%ANDROID_HOME%\tools;%ANDROID_HOME%\platform-tools;`
#### 配置Flutter

1. [下载Flutter](https://flutter.cn/docs/get-started/install)
2. 配置环境
	FLUTTER_HOME: `C:\androidsdk\flutter\bin`
	Path: `%FLUTTER_HOME%`

#### 配置Node

1. [下载nvm-windows-upgrader](https://github.com/coreybutler/nvm-windows/releases)
2. 安装Node `nvm install lts`
3. 配置环境
	NODE_HOME: `C:\Users\xxx\AppData\Roaming\nvm`
	NVM_SYMLINK: `C:\Program Files\nodejs`
	Path: `%NVM_HOME%;%NVM_SYMLINK%`

#### 配置python
1. [下载python](https://www.python.org/downloads/)
2. 配置环境
	PYTHON_HOME: `C:\Users\xxx\AppData\Local\Programs\Python\Python310\`
	Path: `%PYTHON_HOME%;%PYTHON_HOME%\Scripts\`
